PocketCane Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

PocketCane ("the app") is a LiDAR-based navigation tool for iOS. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is shared.

Camera and LiDAR Data

The app uses your device's camera and LiDAR sensor to measure distances and provide haptic feedback in real time. This depth data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted, stored, or logged.

AI Description Features

The "Key Item" and "Environment" buttons capture a photo and send it to the Google Gemini API for analysis. This is the only feature that transmits data off your device. Specifically:

  • A single JPEG image is sent to Google's servers each time you tap one of these buttons.
  • Images are sent only when you explicitly request a description — never automatically.
  • We do not store, log, or retain any images on our servers. We do not operate any servers.
  • Google's use of data sent to the Gemini API is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data We Collect

None. PocketCane does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, analytics, telemetry, location data, or usage statistics. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is the Google Gemini API, as described above. No advertising networks, analytics frameworks, or other third-party SDKs are included in the app.

On-Device Storage

The app stores a single preference on your device (whether you have completed the onboarding flow). No other data is stored.

Children's Privacy

PocketCane is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
